1. Data Usage

All data provided by user in registration is only available to the user and system administrator during the approval process. This data further is used by system administrators in processing customer requests on demand. These services which need the use of user include manual assistance from our team, withdrawing funds to bank account and account approval assistance.

Payment data like VISA card information provided in making payments and deposits is not stored on our system to strengthen your CARD security. You will be asked to re-enter this info whenever you need to make payment because we do not store it.

Data of all your activities (transactions) performed is only available for the user and administrators in activity following instances. It is not possible to share this Information directly on this platform. If you need to share this info, then you will on get it from your browser.

2. Registration and approval

After submitting your application and validating your registered email, Your application is then progressed for review by our team. This is done to ensure that the data provided is valid & correctly arranged because we need this data to process client requests in future. So we review to ensure the application is correctly completed. This action is completed within less than 24HRS. Once approved, you'll receive an email from us and then you can start transacting.

3. Sending and Receiving Funds

Sending and Receiving funds to/fro SD Finance users is free and unlimited. This is mentioned in the head of this page but we wanted to make it very clear "Sending charge is $0 and it is unlimited" Send and Receive as many times as you wish. Available 24/7.

4. Withdrawing

Withdrawing funds from SD Finance account to bank account is charged 1% of amount per transaction. This action is completed within less than 24HRS since it is manually completed by our team to ensure you receive funds on your withdraw account.

Withdraw account are categorized in 3 types at the moment. You can add Bank account, Paypal [email] and Phone number for supported countries. Adding a withdraw account to your list is also approved by our team to ensure you add a correct withdraw A/C. The Withdraw account names and details should match your profile to be approved. Else, you will be contacted by one of our team members to proceed with adding an account which is not in your names. Approval process is completed within 24HRS.

5. Messaging

All conversations and messages in your inbox are only available to the client and his/her recipiet [contact]. 6. Security

All your personal Data, messages, transactions and money is protected by your registered password. This password should only be known by you to ensure the best security of your money and Data. So chose a strong password to protect you funds and Data. It is our advice that if you don't feel secure with current password, please change to maximize your security.
